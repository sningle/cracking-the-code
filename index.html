    What's up with the pistachio?

    Our logo -- a cracked pistachio -- is a play on words with our name, Cracking the Code. Like a pistachio, one of the most difficult parts of the tech industry is initially breaking in to start learning programming and developing your skills. While it can be a tough nut to crack, our goal is to make that process a little easier for beginners by providing informative articles and educational resources accessible to a variety of age groups.
